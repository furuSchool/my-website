## 波動関数

#### 1. 規格化条件：$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\psi(\bm{r},t)|^2d\bm{r} = 1$ すなわち，$\braket{\psi|\psi}=1$

例：$\phi(x)= A \exp(-\frac{x^2}{2\sigma^2})$ の規格化条件
$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|\phi(x)|^2dx &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}A^2\exp(-\frac{x^2}{\sigma^2})dx \\
&= A^2\sqrt{\pi\sigma^2} = 1 \\
\therefore A &= \frac{1}{\pi^{1/4}\sqrt{\sigma}}
\end{aligned}
$$

例：$\phi(x)= A \exp(ikx)~(o < x < L)$ の規格化条件
$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_{0}^{L}|\phi(x)|^2dx &= \int_{0}^{L}A^2\exp(ikx)\exp(-ikx)dx \\
&= A^2L = 1 \\
\therefore A &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{L}}
\end{aligned}
$$

#### 2. 境界条件：一次の微分まで連続，滑らか，有限
例：$x < 0$ で $\psi_{1}(x)$，$0 < x < a$ で $\psi_{2}(x)$，$x > a$ で $\psi_{3}(x)$ の場合
- $\psi_{1}(0) = \psi_{2}(0),~\psi_{2}(a) = \psi_{3}(a)$
- $\psi_{1}'(0) = \psi_{2}'(0),~\psi_{2}'(a) = \psi_{3}'(a)$
- $\psi_1(-\infty) = \psi_3(\infty) = 0$

#### 3. 直交：$\psi(\bm{r},t)$ と $x(\bm{r},t)$ について，$\braket{\psi|x}=0$
